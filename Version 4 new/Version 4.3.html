ABOUT AAROHI

Come winter, the gates to Central India's Largest Cultural Festival are opened ushering in an unprecedented medley of excitement and zeal. The Cultural Extravaganza, studded with electrifying and exhilarating events and competitions, witnesses a whooping footfall and participation. Conceptualized way back in 1988, Aarohi has come a long way in all realms both qualitatively and quantitatively and has firmly ensconced itself as the centerpiece of the cultural spectrum of the region. Aarohi is a Non-Profit organization completely organized and coordinated by the willingness and true spirits of a group of VNITians. Aarohi has truly become one of the most awaited festivals in India. This year we will be celebrating 32nd edition of Aarohi at VNIT, Nagpur.
